Observation:
Qwen produced valid JSON and successfully reranked all 36 planner decision records.
However, for all 30 changed decisions, the Qwen-selected candidate had lower weighted_score and lower explore_ig than the original ActiveSGM-selected candidate.
Several natural-language reasons also contradicted the numeric candidate values.

Conclusion:
Direct Qwen reranking is not safe to apply online yet.
The next step should be a metric-guarded LLM reranker:
- restrict Qwen to high-score candidates;
- enforce minimum weighted_score / explore_ig retention;
- fallback to original ActiveSGM when Qwen selection violates numeric constraints.
